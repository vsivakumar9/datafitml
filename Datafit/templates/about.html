DataFit Project

The DataFit team built tool to analyze a user given data set, perform supervised learning and display predictive models. This utility will be a handy tool in the hands of targeted user personas such as: Analyst, Strategist, Decision Makers, etc.
🗸 We analyzed e-commerce data of Industry-wide apparel orders and its specifications. Our website was built in HTML, Bootstrap 4, utilizing JS, JQuery and AJAX for navigation controls. Descriptive visualizations were built in Tableau.
🗸 We prioritized our modeling design to provide a working prototype of a single use case, that enables upload of custom data and displays an evaluation
🗸 We plan to provide many more useful statistical models for predictive analysis - the NEXT milestone on our roadmap!
