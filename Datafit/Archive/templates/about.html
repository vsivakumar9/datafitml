DataDash Project

The Datadash team scraped job description data from Glassdoor.com. We stored and cleaned data in SQLite.

We analyzed text for key words using natural language processing and used raw data to evaluate the requirements for 3 specific job titles. Website was built in Bootstrap 4, utilizing JQuery and AJAX for navigation controls. Visualizations were built in Plotly and Carto.

Next on our roadmap is to integrate the resume analysis tool - job seeker pastes the test of their resume on the website, and get a recommended job description.
